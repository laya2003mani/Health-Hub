Intermediate Plan

For those with some experience

30-45 min/day 6 weeks

Cardio

  • Jogging (25 mins) (Maintain conversational pace, land mid-foot) ≈250 kcal
  • Cycling intervals (5x1 min fast/2 min slow) (Increase resistance during fast intervals) ≈200 kcal
  • Elliptical (20 mins) (Use handles for full-body workout) ≈180 kcal

Strength

  • Dumbbell squats (3x12) (Hold weights at shoulders, keep chest up) ≈120 kcal
  • Push-ups (3x12) (Keep body straight, lower chest to floor) ≈90 kcal
  • Bent-over rows (3x10) (Hinge at hips, pull weights to waist) ≈100 kcal

Circuit

  • 3-round circuit: Squats (15), Push-ups (10), Plank (30 sec) (Minimal rest between exercises) ≈150 kcal
  • 3-round circuit: Lunges (10 each), Dumbbell press (12), Bicycle crunches (20) (30 sec rest between rounds) ≈180 kcal

Strength

  • Pull-ups (3x max reps) (Start from dead hang, pull chin over bar) ≈150 kcal
  • Dumbbell complex (Squat to press, 3x10) (Combine movements fluidly) ≈180 kcal

Endurance

  • 5K run (Maintain steady pace, focus on form) ≈350 kcal
  • Swimming (30 mins continuous) (Alternate strokes every 5 laps) ≈300 kcal
