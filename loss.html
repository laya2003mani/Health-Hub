Beginner Program

Perfect for those just starting out

20-30 min/day 4 weeks

Cardio

  • Brisk walking (20 mins) ≈120 kcal - Fast-paced walking to gently raise heart rate
  • Cycling (15 mins) ≈150 kcal - Riding a bike at moderate pace
  • Jump rope (5 sets of 30 sec) ≈100 kcal - Short rope jumping bursts with rests
  • Jogging in place (3x45 sec) ≈90 kcal - Gentle running without moving forward
  • Step-ups (3x10 per leg) ≈80 kcal - Stepping up and down on a low surface

Strength

  • Bodyweight squats (3x15) ≈75 kcal - Standing up/down like sitting in a chair
  • Wall push-ups (3x12) ≈60 kcal - Easier push-ups leaning against a wall
  • Plank (3x20 sec) ≈45 kcal - Holding push-up position to strengthen core
  • Lunges (3x10 per leg) ≈90 kcal - Stepping forward and bending both knees
  • Glute bridges (3x12) ≈60 kcal - Lifting hips while lying on your back

Flexibility

  • Seated toe touch (3x15 sec) ≈30 kcal - Sitting and reaching for toes to stretch legs
  • Cat-Cow stretch (2x10 reps) ≈25 kcal - Arching and rounding back while on hands/knees
  • Child's pose (30 sec hold) ≈15 kcal - Kneeling with arms stretched forward to relax back
  • Neck stretches (2x15 sec) ≈20 kcal - Gently tilting head side to side to release tension

Intermediate Plan

For those with some experience

30-45 min/day 6 weeks

Cardio

  • Jogging (25 mins) ≈250 kcal - Continuous running at moderate pace
  • Stair climbing (10 mins) ≈150 kcal - Going up/down stairs for leg endurance
  • Swimming (15 mins) ≈200 kcal - Full-body workout in water
  • Cycling intervals (5x30 sec) ≈180 kcal - Alternating fast/slow biking bursts

Strength

  • Dumbbell rows (3x12) ≈90 kcal - Bending and pulling weights to work back muscles
  • Lunges (3x10 per leg) ≈120 kcal - Stepping forward with weights (optional)
  • Push-ups (3x12) ≈80 kcal - Classic floor push-ups for chest/arms
  • Dumbbell shoulder press (3x10) ≈75 kcal - Lifting weights overhead for shoulders
  • Romanian deadlifts (3x10) ≈100 kcal - Bending forward with weights, keeping legs straight

HIIT

  • Jump squats (30 sec on/30 sec off x5) ≈150 kcal - Explosive squats with jumps between
  • Burpees (8 reps x 3 sets) ≈120 kcal - Full-body movement: squat, push-up, jump
  • Mountain climbers (45 sec x3) ≈100 kcal - Running knees to chest in plank position
  • High knees (30 sec on/30 sec off x5) ≈140 kcal - Running in place lifting knees high

Advanced Challenge

High-intensity fat burning

45-60 min/day 8 weeks

HIIT

  • Sprint intervals (30 sec sprint/1 min walk x10) ≈300 kcal - All-out running bursts with recovery walks
  • Box jumps (4x12) ≈180 kcal - Jumping onto a sturdy box/platform
  • Kettlebell swings (3x15) ≈150 kcal - Swinging weight between legs and up to chest
  • Battle ropes (30 sec on/60 sec off x5) ≈200 kcal - Waving thick ropes for upper-body power

Strength

  • Deadlifts (4x8) ≈200 kcal - Lifting heavy bar from ground to hip level
  • Pull-ups (3x max reps) ≈120 kcal - Hanging and pulling body up
  • Bench press (4x10) ≈180 kcal - Lifting barbell while lying on bench
  • Overhead press (4x8) ≈150 kcal - Pressing weights straight overhead

Endurance

  • 5K run (sub-25 min target) ≈350 kcal - Running 5 kilometers at fast pace
  • Rowing machine (2000m time trial) ≈250 kcal - Full-body rowing for distance
  • Circuit training (5 rounds) ≈400 kcal - Multiple exercises back-to-back with little rest

Injury Recovery

  • Knee stability (3x12) ≈50 kcal - Exercises to support weak knees

Senior Fitness

  • Seated leg extensions (3x12) ≈60 kcal - Straightening legs while sitting to strengthen
  • Arm circles (3x15) ≈40 kcal - Small arm circles to improve mobility
